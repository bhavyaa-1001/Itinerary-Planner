Expert-Level Destination Insights

Paris, France

Must-Visit Attractions: Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, Notre Dame Cathedral, Montmartre.

Local Customs: Greet with a kiss on both cheeks, respect meal times, dress elegantly.

Transportation Options: Metro, buses, taxis, bike rentals.

Dining Recommendations: Le Jules Verne, L'Ambroisie, Le Meurice, Café de Flore.

Rome, Italy

Must-Visit Attractions: Colosseum, Vatican Museums, Trevi Fountain, Pantheon.

Local Customs: Dress modestly in churches, avoid eating on the go, greet with a handshake.

Transportation Options: Metro, buses, trams, taxis.

Dining Recommendations: La Pergola, Roscioli, Trattoria Da Enzo, Pizzarium.

Beijing, China

Must-Visit Attractions: Great Wall of China, Forbidden City, Temple of Heaven, Summer Palace.

Local Customs: Bow slightly when greeting, avoid pointing with your finger, respect elders.

Transportation Options: Subway, buses, taxis, bike rentals.

Dining Recommendations: Da Dong Roast Duck, TRB Hutong, Din Tai Fung, Quanjude.

Sydney, Australia

Must-Visit Attractions: Sydney Opera House, Sydney Harbour Bridge, Bondi Beach, Taronga Zoo.

Local Customs: Greet with a handshake, respect personal space, be punctual.

Transportation Options: Trains, buses, ferries, taxis.

Dining Recommendations: Quay, Bennelong, Tetsuya's, Rockpool Bar & Grill.

Tokyo, Japan

Must-Visit Attractions: Tokyo Tower, Senso-ji Temple, Meiji Shrine, Shibuya Crossing.

Local Customs: Bow when greeting, remove shoes indoors, avoid loud conversations in public.

Transportation Options: Trains, subways, buses, taxis.

Dining Recommendations: Sukiyabashi Jiro, Sushi Saito, Narisawa, Ichiran Ramen.

New York City, USA

Must-Visit Attractions: Times Square, Central Park, Statue of Liberty, Empire State Building.

Local Customs: Greet with a handshake, tip service workers, be mindful of personal space.

Transportation Options: Subway, buses, taxis, bike rentals.

Dining Recommendations: Le Bernardin, Katz's Delicatessen, Peter Luger Steak House, Joe's Pizza.
